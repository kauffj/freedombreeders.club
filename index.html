Welcome to the Freedom Breeder's Club

We're a group of parents raising children in the Free State of New Hampshire. Our principles:

  • Children should be free to explore, pursue, and master their own interests, through both play and intentional learning.
  • Children should acquire pro-liberty skills such as logic, fitness, teamwork, science, self-defense, and self-sufficiency.
  • Children can often do more than you think, if you let them (and raise them the right way).

We love meeting other pro-liberty families in New Hampshire or that are New Hampshire curious. Contact us at hello@freedombreeders.club
